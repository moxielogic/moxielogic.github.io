New Site

I had finally had enough of the wiki spammers, and decided to migrate all of my content over to a static github hosted site. I believe all of the blog entries migrated over now, and I'm in the process of migrating the wiki content over to pages you can link to from the sidebar (see Architecture, for instance).

If I ever get around to learning how htaccess rewrite rules work, I should be able to redirect RSS feed subscribers to the new link. But until then, nobody will get any new entries in their readers.

Once I get this out of the way, I'll post something new and amazing about moxie.
